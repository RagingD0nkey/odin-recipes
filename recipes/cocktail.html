The REAL Long Island Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 15ml vodka
  • 15ml rum
  • 15ml gin
  • 15ml tequila
  • 15ml triple-sec
  • 30ml sweet and sour mix
  • Top with cola
  • 1 lemon slice

Directions

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice; cover and shake.
  2. Pour cocktail into a Collins or hurricane glass; top with splash of cola for color.
  3. Garnish with a lemon slice.
