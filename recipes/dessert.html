Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup key lime juice
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and egg yolks in a large bowl; mix well.
  3. Pour mixture into unbaked graham cracker crust.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, about 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool completely before slicing.
